    This is a contact / php mail form script that uses reCAPTCHA to verify that a human is submitting it.

    I got tired of bots emailing me through contact forms, so I made one that uses reCAPTCHA... I bet many people have already made one, but I figured that I might as well release it since it took me some time to make. :)

    Feel free to help out on it. If you wish to contact the author, he can be reached at danielc [at] tocloud [dot] us.

    To use it on your own site: Drop it into a folder, such as /var/www/vhosts/myvhost.com/htdocs/contact Modify the "settings" array in the file common.php to match your wanted settings.

    ** Be sure to remove the sanity line from the settings array. :) **

    Thanks to http://beta.phpformatter.com/ for helping me get my code human readable.
